ABOUT THE COMPANY

Engineered in India.
Localized in Canada.

Mahakali Rubber Industries operates as one integrated manufacturing network across two continents, pairing deep compounding expertise with a growing North American footprint for faster regional delivery to OEM lines.

India — Global Headquarters

100% IN-HOUSE MANUFACTURING

Ahmedabad, Gujarat is where compounding, mixing, moulding, extrusion, curing, finishing and quality testing happen under one roof. As our Global Headquarters, India manufactures the full catalogue of Mahakali components shipped worldwide.

  • End-to-end in-house production line
  • In-house tooling & mould design
  • Bulk export capability

Canada — Calgary, AB Facility

MIXING · MOULDING · SALES

Our Calgary, Alberta facility brings mixing and moulding capacity closer to North American procurement teams — cutting lead times, simplifying logistics, and supporting local technical sales and after-sales response.

  • Local sales & technical support

Engineered Component Categories

Gaskets & Seals

Die-cut and compression-moulded gaskets in NBR, Viton, Silicone and EPDM for fluid-sealing applications.

O-Rings

Precision-moulded sealing rings in AS568 and metric sizes for hydraulic and pneumatic assemblies.

Bushings

Vibration-damping bushings and mounts moulded to precise load and deflection curves.

Bellows

Convoluted flexible bellows for expansion joints, dust boots and protective covers.

Rubber Profiles

Extruded seals, edge trims and gasket profiles cut and spliced to length.

Rubber-to-Metal Bonded Parts

Bonded mounts and inserts engineered for permanent adhesion under load and thermal cycling.

Rubber Sheets — Sub-Category

SBR, Nitrile, EPDM, Neoprene and Natural rubber sheets in smooth, ribbed or diamond-tread finishes — Sh 40–90A, 1–50mm, slit or rolled to size. Available on request alongside any custom component order.
