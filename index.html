ABOUT THE COMPANY

Engineered in India.
Expanded to Canada.

Mahakali Rubber Industries operates as one integrated manufacturing network across two continents, combining deep compounding expertise with a growing global footprint for faster regional delivery.

India — Global Headquarters

100% IN-HOUSE MANUFACTURING

Ahmedabad, Gujarat is where it all happens — compounding, mixing, moulding, extrusion, curing, finishing and quality testing under one roof, entirely in-house. As our Global Headquarters, India manufactures the full catalogue of Mahakali products shipped worldwide.

  • End-to-end in-house production line
  • In-house tooling & mould design
  • Bulk export capability

Canada — Calgary, AB Facility

MIXING · MOULDING · SALES

Our newly expanded Calgary, Alberta facility brings mixing and moulding capacity closer to North American clients — reducing lead times, easing logistics, and supporting local technical sales and after-sales support.

  • Regional mixing & moulding
  • Faster North American turnaround
  • Local sales & technical support

WHO WE SERVE

Industries We Serve

Our compounds are formulated to meet the operating demands of six major industrial sectors.

Automotive

Seals, bushings, mounts and gaskets built for thermal and chemical resistance.

Aerospace

Tight-tolerance seals and dampening components for demanding flight environments.

Industrial Manufacturing

Machine gaskets, anti-vibration mounts and wear parts for production lines.

Mining

Heavy-duty conveyor lining and belting engineered for abrasion and impact.

Agriculture

Weatherproof seals, matting and moulded parts for farm equipment.

Marine

Salt-water and UV-resistant fendering, seals and deck components.

Not seeing your industry?

We formulate custom rubber compounds for specialized applications outside our core sectors too. Send us your requirement and our engineers will assess feasibility.
